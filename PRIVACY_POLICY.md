# BRE4CH Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 6, 2026

## Overview

BRE4CH ("Battlefield Real-time Event Assessment & Crisis Hub") is an operational intelligence dashboard for real-time crisis monitoring. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- BRE4CH collects precise location data **only when you explicitly enable** the GPS navigation feature.
- Location data is used solely for on-device navigation and distance calculations.
- Location data is **not transmitted** to our servers or any third parties.

### Push Notification Tokens
- We collect Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to deliver critical operational alerts.
- Tokens are stored securely on our servers and used exclusively for push notifications.

### Usage Data
- We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the application.
- No personally identifiable information is collected.

## Data Storage

- All cached data (maps, intelligence reports, OSINT feeds) is stored locally on your device.
- Server-side data is hosted on secure infrastructure (Hetzner, Germany) and protected with industry-standard encryption.

## Third-Party Services

BRE4CH uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** (Google) — for push notifications
- **OpenStreetMap / CartoDB** — for map tiles
- **RSS Feed Aggregation** — for open-source intelligence

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Retention

- Local cache data can be cleared at any time through the app settings.
- FCM tokens are retained only while the app is installed.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- HTTPS encryption for all API communications
- Certificate pinning for sensitive endpoints
- Local data encryption via Hive

## Children's Privacy

BRE4CH is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
